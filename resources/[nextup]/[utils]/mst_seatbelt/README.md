## 🛠 Support & Policy

We are committed to providing responsive and reliable support for all Mosoto Scripts customers.  
Support is available via our official Discord community, where our team can help you troubleshoot and customize your setup.

If you still need help, have the following ready:

- **Script Name & Version** – The exact name of the Mosoto Scripts product and version number.
- **Detailed Description** – What happened, and when?
- **Troubleshooting Steps Taken** – What have you already tried?
- **Error Messages / Screenshots** – Enable Config.Debug. This will help us identify the problem faster.

- **Hours**: Monday - Friday (limited weekend availability)
- **Response Time**: Typically within 12 hours
- **Respect**: Please remain respectful – we’re here to help you succeed.

💬 **Support Community**: [Join our Discord](https://discord.mosotoscripts.com)
